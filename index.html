  •     I must have lived before
    or been free once
    because nothing fills me more
    with shattering nostalgia than the night sky
    and the scenic views as we drive
    past fenced off stretches of land,
    sunflowers and bluebonnets lighting the way
    to somewhere we don't know but we would like to believe we do,
    and the graves that are sprinkled along the way
    to the cities speak volumes of their wanting to be known
    in life so silent, they now scream in their graves
    as the brilliant sun warms them with it's bright golden drops
    reminding us that it's only once we're in the city
    that we miss the drive
    and the quiet star filled skies at night
    with those that will always mean
    the most.

    February 2011
